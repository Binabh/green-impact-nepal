Campaign Overview

In celebration of International Biodiversity Day, Green Impact Nepal launched an innovative digital campaign featuring a creative reel competition designed to raise awareness about biodiversity conservation and environmental protection. This engaging initiative harnessed the power of social media to reach younger audiences and inspire action for biodiversity preservation.

International Biodiversity Day, observed annually on May 22nd, provides a global platform to increase understanding and awareness of biodiversity issues. Through this campaign, we aimed to highlight the incredible richness of Nepal's biodiversity while encouraging creative expression and environmental advocacy among participants.

Creative Expression for Conservation

The reel competition invited participants to create short, impactful videos showcasing Nepal's diverse ecosystems, wildlife species, and conservation efforts. Participants were encouraged to use their creativity to tell compelling stories about biodiversity, from the towering peaks of the Himalayas to the lush forests of the Terai region.

The campaign successfully engaged content creators, students, environmental enthusiasts, and the general public in meaningful conversations about biodiversity conservation. By leveraging popular social media formats, we were able to make environmental education more accessible and engaging for diverse audiences across Nepal and beyond.

Digital Innovation for Environmental Awareness

This biodiversity awareness campaign demonstrated the effectiveness of combining traditional conservation messaging with modern digital platforms and creative formats. By encouraging user-generated content, we not only spread awareness but also empowered individuals to become advocates for biodiversity conservation in their own communities. The campaign's success in engaging diverse audiences highlights the importance of innovative approaches to environmental education and the power of social media in driving positive change for our planet's precious biodiversity.
